Profesional Profile

Born and raised in New York City, I've always loved cities -- the energy, the surprises, the diversity of experiences. I'm particularly interested in how cities keep residents safe from crime, disorder, and terrorism. I spent the last few years working in law enforcement for the City of New York where I've supported criminal investigations, conducted large data analytics projects and helped devise crime strategies. I've found that I'm most passionate about exploring the intersection of public safety and technology.

Currently, I serve as the Lead for Public Safety & Criminal Justice at Sidewalk Labs, an Alphabet company that works with cities to address big urban problems.
